# Privacy Policy for Taboom

**Last updated: April 5, 2026**

## Overview

Taboom ("we", "our", or "us") is a word card game for iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

## Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. We do not require account registration or login.

## Advertising

Taboom displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

- Device identifiers (e.g. Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Coarse location (country/region)
- App usage data

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit:  
[https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

## App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS, you may be asked for permission to track your activity across apps and websites. You can change this at any time in **Settings → Privacy → Tracking**.

## Data Storage

App preferences (language, sound settings) are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to us.

## Children's Privacy

Taboom is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
